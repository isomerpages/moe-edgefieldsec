<p><strong>Description</strong> 
<br>Shooting is a CCA that requires mental toughness, strong upper body and
core strength. During the CCA sessions, shooters develop values of discipline
and focus through tough trainings to prepare them for local competitions.
Shooters also practice sports psychological skills such as relaxation techniques,
concentration techniques and performance routines. These essential skills
help shooters to perform at the optimum level during competitions.</p>
<p>Shooting CCA hopes to cultivate a “Disciplined Mind” in the students through
the learning of proper shooting techniques and training.</p>
