<br><strong>Description</strong> 
<br>
</p>
<p><em>"Basketball is more than a game; it's a test of heart, mind, and teamwork."</em>
</p>
<p>As a Co-Curricular Activity (CCA), basketball aims to not only strengthen
students physically but also nurture their mental fortitude. Through regular
training and competitions, we instill values of commitment, self-discipline,
and resilience, shaping students to persevere in the face of challenges.
The CCA strives to cultivate the spirit of sportsmanship and teamwork,
while also emphasizing the pursuit of excellence. We believe that winning
goes beyond trophies—it's about the relentless pursuit of growth, both
as individuals and as a team.</p>
