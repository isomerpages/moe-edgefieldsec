<p><strong>Description</strong> 
<br>Volleyball instills within its members an acute awareness of effective
teamwork. The players undergo physical skills training that also increases
their fitness levels, and also the soft skills of communication, and the
values of resoluteness and perseverance. The CCA hopes to cultivate the
“Disciplined Mind” through its rigorous training in the physical and mental
domains of the players.</p>
