<p><strong>Description</strong> 
<br>Festive Drums CCA hopes to cultivate a “Synthesizing Mind” and “Disciplined
Mind” in the students through drumming.
<br>It is not solely on acquiring the correct rhythm, it is also about coordination
and teamwork.
<br>Through drumming, we hope to instil the values of respect, harmony and
responsibility towards one another.</p>
