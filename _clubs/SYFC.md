<p><strong>Description</strong> 
<br>Singapore Youth Flying Club (SYFC) is a co-curricular activity that introduces
secondary school students to aviation through activities like glider construction,
flight simulators, and drone flying. The CCA develops teamwork, discipline,
and problem-solving skills, while nurturing interest in aerospace and flight.</p>
