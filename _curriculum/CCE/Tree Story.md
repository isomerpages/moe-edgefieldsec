<p>At the heart of our Student Development approach is the belief that every
Edgefielder has the potential to grow into an individual who contributes
meaningfully to the future of our nation.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Our Student Development model is anchored in the metaphor of a tree,
where students are firmly rooted in a caring and enabling school environment
that empowers them to build strong relationships, foster school connectedness,
and develop a values-based sense of purpose.&nbsp;At the core of our work
lies intentionality and student-centricity,&nbsp;guiding us to create a
coherent and meaningful experience that supports students through their
formative years.</p>
